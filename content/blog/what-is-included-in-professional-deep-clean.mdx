A professional deep clean is one of the most comprehensive home maintenance investments you can make. Unlike a regular maintenance clean — which focuses on keeping things tidy — a deep clean tackles every corner, crevice, and built-up layer of grime that accumulates over time.

But what exactly do we mean when we say "deep clean"? Here's a complete breakdown.

## Regular Clean vs Deep Clean: What's the Difference?

| Feature | Regular Clean | Deep Clean |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Frequency | Weekly / fortnightly | Seasonal / once-off |
| Oven interior | ✗ | ✓ |
| Fridge interior | ✗ | ✓ |
| Inside cupboards | ✗ | ✓ |
| Window tracks | Sometimes | ✓ |
| Grout scrubbing | ✗ | ✓ |
| Behind appliances | ✗ | ✓ |
| Walls | Spot only | Full wash |
| Duration | 2–3 hours | 4–8 hours |

## What's Included in a Professional Deep Clean?

### Kitchen — The Most Labour-Intensive Area

The kitchen accumulates the most grime and is usually where the most time is spent in a deep clean.

- **Oven**: Cleaned inside and out, including racks, door glass, and cavity walls
- **Rangehood**: Filters degreased or replaced, exterior wiped down
- **Fridge**: Interior shelves, drawers, and door seals cleaned
- **Cupboards**: All doors, hinges, and interiors wiped down
- **Stovetop and burners**: Grease removed, burner grates soaked and scrubbed
- **Benchtops**: Scrubbed and sanitised including edges and backsplash
- **Sink**: Descaled, polished, drain cleaned

### Bathrooms — Hygiene Priority

- **Tiles and grout**: Scrubbed and treated (grout brightening included)
- **Shower screen**: Calcium and soap scum removed
- **Toilet**: Inside bowl, under rim, behind toilet, seat and hinges
- **Exhaust fan**: Cover removed and cleaned
- **Vanity and storage**: All surfaces wiped, including shelves inside cabinets
- **Mirrors**: Polished streak-free

### Living Areas

- **Walls**: Washed down for marks, fingerprints, and scuffs
- **Skirting boards**: Scrubbed — not just dusted
- **Light fittings**: Removed and cleaned (ceiling fans included)
- **Window sills and tracks**: Scrubbed and vacuumed
- **Blinds**: Each slat wiped or vacuumed
- **Under and behind furniture**: Moved (where safe to do so) and cleaned beneath
- **Air vents and return air grilles**: Dusted and wiped

### Bedrooms

- **Wardrobe interiors**: Shelves, rails, and floor vacuumed
- **Ceiling fan blades**: Wiped down
- **Under-bed area**: Vacuumed thoroughly
- **Door handles and light switches**: Sanitised
- **Carpets**: Deep vacuum (steam cleaning available as add-on)

## When Should You Book a Deep Clean?

Deep cleans are ideal for:

- **Moving into a new home** — even new builds can have construction dust and residue
- **Spring cleaning** — resetting the home before the warmer months
- **Post-renovation** — dust and debris from building work gets everywhere
- **Before/after hosting a major event**
- **When a property needs a complete reset** — if regular cleaning has slipped

## How Long Does a Deep Clean Take?

Expect:
- **1–2 bedroom home**: 4–5 hours
- **3–4 bedroom home**: 6–7 hours
- **5+ bedroom home**: 8+ hours

Timing varies based on property condition. A home that hasn't had a thorough clean in some time will naturally take longer.
